# Rule-Based Router

- Route to `pubmed` for literature, paper, journal, study, PubMed, or NCBI requests.
- Route to `quiz` for study questions, MCQs, flashcards, or testing requests.
- Route to `simplify` for plain-language or simplification requests.
- Route to `summarize` for summary or key-point requests.
- Route to `prompt_enhance` only for explicit prompt-improvement requests.
- Otherwise route to `rag`.
